Calculation notes

What this estimate assumes.

Fixed rate, no recast
Principal and interest stay on the original 30-year schedule; this estimate does not recast the regular payment after extras.
Lender handling varies
Extras are applied after the scheduled payment. Lender rounding, payment order, recasting, and prepayment rules can change the result slightly.
Estimate scope
Taxes, insurance, fees, tax effects, and alternative uses of the extra cash are excluded. Results are based only on your inputs.

Mortgage Paydown is an educational estimate, not financial advice. Confirm payment rules with your lender.
