Calculation notes

What this estimate assumes.

Fixed-rate loan
Interest accrues monthly on the remaining principal over a 30-year term.
Extras reduce principal
Additional payments do not replace the normal payment and stop when the loan is paid off.
Housing costs excluded
Taxes, homeowners insurance, mortgage insurance or PMI, HOA fees, closing costs, and prepayment penalties are not included.
