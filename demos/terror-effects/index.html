    Coverage & methodology

    Circles aggregate geocoded incidents to 0.25° cell centers; they do not mark exact locations. Circle area is approximately proportional to incident count, and orange intensity reinforces that same frequency measure. This does not encode the severity of an individual attack. Open a cell for the exact count.

    Blank casualty values remain unknown, not zero. “Confirmed fatalities” sums only reported values and includes victims and perpetrators under GTD conventions.

    2021 covers January–June only. Collection methods and variable availability changed over time, so annual differences can partly reflect methodology.
