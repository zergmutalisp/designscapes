    What the comparison measures

    GTD records selected terrorist incidents. UCDP records organized violence using a different event system. Eurostat records applications by citizenship and reporting destination. These measures are kept separate.

    What it does not establish

    Temporal alignment does not establish that recorded terrorism caused an application. Conflict, policy, economic conditions, routes, and existing migration networks may shape both timing and destination.
